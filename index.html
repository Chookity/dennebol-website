Card Game Adventure
A group of friends coming together to create an amazing new 3D card game.
The game will be crossplatform, available on Android, iOS and PC devices.
A free card game adventure with additional cosmetic upgrades

Go head to head with your opponent trying to come up on top.
Choose a faction whose army will accompany you into battle as you face your foes.

Whether it's goblins or elves, mages or beasts, each faction will define which allies you can summon.
Drain the lifepoints of your opponent before he finishes you off!

Come joins us in an epic adventure!
